Overview

The Ministry of Justice, Legal Affairs, and Constitutional Affairs is responsible for the administration of justice and the development and implementation of legal policies and programs in Kenya. The ministry aims to promote access to justice, protection of human rights, and adherence to the rule of law. Its functions include legal policy formulation, development and review of legislation, management of the justice system, provision of legal advice to the government, and promotion of alternative dispute resolution mechanisms. The ministry also works closely with various stakeholders, including the judiciary, legal profession, civil society, and development partners, to achieve its objectives.
