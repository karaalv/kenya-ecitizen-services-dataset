The Senate

The role of the Senate includes the exercise of the legislative authority of the Republic, manifest of the diversity of the nation, representation of the will of the people, and exercise their sovereignty, consideration, and passing of amendments to this Constitution, alteration of county boundaries, and protection of the Constitution and promotion of the democratic governance of the Republic.

The State Department for East African Community

The State Department for East African Community is charged with the responsibility of coordinating and implementing policies, strategies and programmes aimed at deepening regional integration. It formulates policy on East African Community, coordinates Government and private sector participation in the EAC Affairs, coordinates the implementation of the EAC regional programmes, coordinates the implementation of EAC Treaty and promotes and fast tracks EAC integration process.

The State Department for Youth Affairs and Creative Economy

The State Department for Youth Affairs and Creative Economy is mandated to provide leadership, coordination, and an enabling environment for the development of youth and creatives empowerment programmes. This programmes include Innovation, Talent and creative industry development, Youth Social development, Entrepreneurship and Skills development as well as policy, research and mainstreaming.

Ulinzi Health Services Fund ( UHSF)

Ulinzi Health Services Fund ( UHSF)  is a government-established fund that finances the provision, modernization and sustainable operation of medical and health services at Kenya Defence Forces Level IV, V and VI hospital facilities, using income from services, appropriations, grants and other lawful sources. Veterinary Medicines Directorate

The Veterinary Medicines Directorate (VMD) is an agency tasked with regulating the manufacture, importation, exportation, registration, distribution, prescription and dispensing of veterinary medicines and other animal health products in Kenya. VMD was established pursuant to CAP 366 (article 39) of the laws of Kenya

Water Resources Authority

Water Resources Authority (WRA) regulates water abstractions for different uses while ensuring equitable sharing. The process involves submitting an application, giving authorization to construct abstraction works, followed by the issuance of a water permit.

Water Sector Trust Fund (WaterFund) provides conditional and unconditional grants to Counties and assists in financing the development of and management of water and sanitation services in the marginalized and underserved areas. Its mandate includes research activities in the area of water resource management and water services, sewerage and sanitation

Witness Protection Agency

The object and purpose of the Witness Protection Agency is to provide the framework and procedures for giving special protection, on behalf of the State, to persons in possession of important information and who are facing potential risk or intimidation due to their co-operation with the prosecution and other law enforcement agencies as stated in Section 3B, Witness Protection Act.
